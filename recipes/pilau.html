Beef Pilau Rice

Beef pilau rice is a rich and flavourful one pot dish recipe fit for a weeknight.
This classic pilau rice is a hit at parties BBQs and with kids and adults alike.

It comes easily and is a perfect main for summer parties and get togethers.

Ingredients

  • Ghee
  • Pilau masala spice
  • Basmati rise
  • Beef(washed and ut into bite pieces)
  • Onions
  • Garlic-ginger paste
  • Tomatoes
  • Seasoning powder or cubes
  • Water
  • Bay leaves
  • Pilau masala
  • Beef broth
  • Coriander leaves
  • Coriander powder
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Heat ghee or cooking oil on medium heat
  2. Add onions and fry till golden brown. The browner the onions the darker the rice
  3. Add the garli-ginger paste and sautee for a while
  4. Add the beef and fry till brown for about 10 minutes
  5. Proceed to add the chopped tomatoes, salt, pilau masala, coriander powder, seasoning powder and chilli powder and sautee
  6. Cover the pot and allow the tomatoes to cook for at least 6 minutes.Stir occasionally.
  7. Add one cup of water, cover the pot and let the meat simmer for at least 15 minutes on medium heat until almost all the liquid has evaporated
  8. After that add the potatoes, washed rice and hot water.Stir to mix all the ingredients.
  9. Cover the pot then steam the rice for about 30 minutes or until fully cooked
  10. After 30 minutes, turn off the heat and allow the rice to rest for about 15minutes
  11. Add the finely chopped coriander leaves and fluff the rice from top to bottom.
  12. Serve the rice on its own or garnished with some coriander leaves
